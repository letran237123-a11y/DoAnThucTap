During my internship at AWS Company from April 17, 2026 to July 10, 2026, I had the opportunity to learn, practice, and apply the knowledge I gained at university in a real working environment.

I participated in an action-adventure game project featuring boss battles with both online and offline modes. My primary responsibilities focused on developing the game's user interface, including implementing basic UI elements such as the health bar, inventory slots, and weapon information display, as well as designing scenes such as the Main Menu and Lobby Scene. Through this project, I improved my game programming skills, code analysis abilities, UI design and implementation skills, and the ability to develop UI logic. In addition, through the AWS training program, I gained practical experience with core AWS services such as AWS Billing and Cost Management, Amazon CloudWatch, Amazon EC2, Amazon RDS, AWS Support, and monitoring tools. These activities helped me better understand cloud computing concepts and how AWS services are applied in real-world scenarios. I also enhanced my communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and technical report writing skills.

Regarding my working attitude, I always tried to complete assigned tasks responsibly, complied with the company's rules and regulations, and actively communicated with my teammates as well as other interns in the company to improve work efficiency.
