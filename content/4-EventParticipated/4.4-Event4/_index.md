# Summary Report: “AWS Cloud Career & AI Readiness”

### Event Objectives

- Explore career trends in the field of Cloud Computing.
- Understand the gap between university knowledge and real-world industry requirements.
- Learn how to use AI effectively while maintaining critical thinking and fundamental knowledge.
- Improve communication skills, proactiveness, and personal development.

### Speakers

The event featured the following speakers:

#### Nguyen Gia Hung – Head of Solutions Architecture at AWS Vietnam

- Shared insights into the growth of the Cloud market in Vietnam.
- Analyzed current hiring requirements and the skills students should prepare.
- Encouraged students to build projects that solve real business problems.

#### Banh Cam Vinh – Data Engineer at Renova Cloud

- Explained the differences between academic learning and real-world work.
- Emphasized the importance of databases, programming, and data structures.
- Shared practical approaches to building projects that closely resemble production environments.

#### Nhu Tran – Account Manager at AWS Vietnam

- Shared strategies for recognizing and overcoming fear.
- Encouraged students to communicate proactively and not be afraid of making mistakes.
- Highlighted the importance of understanding personal strengths and developing one's unique potential.

#### Anh Khang – Swinburne University Alumnus

- Shared learning and career experiences in the Cloud industry.
- Discussed the advantages and limitations of using AI for learning.
- Encouraged students to use AI as a tool to support thinking rather than relying on it completely.

### Key Highlights

#### 1. Cloud Career Trends

- Cloud Computing has become a key technology adopted by many businesses.
- Students need strong fundamental knowledge, practical skills, and an understanding of real-world applications.

#### 2. The Gap Between University and Industry

- Real-world projects often involve complex data, changing requirements, and tight deadlines.
- Students should pay attention to security, error handling, data quality, and system reliability.

#### 3. Communication Skills and Personal Development

- Students should actively ask questions, share their ideas, and avoid being afraid of criticism.
- Continuous practice helps improve confidence and communication skills.

#### 4. Using AI Effectively

- AI can accelerate learning and productivity, but it cannot replace fundamental knowledge.
- Users should understand, verify, and evaluate AI-generated results before applying them.

### Impact on My Internship

- Gained a clearer understanding of recruitment expectations in the Cloud and technology industry.
- Recognized the importance of practical projects and strong foundational knowledge.
- Learned how to use AI responsibly to support learning and project development.

### Community Networking Activities

- Interacted with AWS experts and technology engineers through Q&A sessions.
- Exchanged experiences on studying, internships, and career development.
- Expanded my professional network with students and members of the Cloud community.

### Future Development Plan

- Continue learning and practicing AWS services.
- Build projects that closely resemble real-world production environments.
- Improve communication, teamwork, and presentation skills.
- Use AI as a supporting tool rather than relying on it completely.
