# Summary Report: “FCAJ Community Day”

### Event Objectives

- Share practical knowledge about modern software development and AWS technologies.
- Introduce AI-driven development tools and enterprise solutions.
- Provide career guidance and industry experience from experienced speakers.
- Encourage continuous learning and collaboration within the developer community.

### Speakers

The workshop featured several experienced speakers from the AWS and software development community.

**Master Hưng**

**Topic: "Three Essential Elements for Modern Developers"**

- Highlighted the importance of strong technical foundations.
- Emphasized continuous learning and practical working experience.
- Encouraged developers to focus on mastering fundamentals before building products.

**Tịnh**

**Topic: "Why AI Fails Without Context"**

- Explained the importance of providing sufficient context when using AI.
- Introduced concepts such as AI-first thinking, prompt engineering, and memory.
- Shared common mistakes that reduce AI performance.

**Anh Phạm**

**Topic: "Amazon Q Overview and Demo"**

- Introduced the capabilities of Amazon Q.
- Demonstrated how Amazon Q assists software development.
- Shared practical examples of integrating Amazon Q with AWS services.

**Thịnh**

**Topic: "CloudFront Fixed Pricing and Advanced Security"**

- Explained CloudFront pricing models and cost optimization strategies.
- Introduced security features including DDoS protection, HTTPS compression, Signed URLs, and VPC Origin.
- Demonstrated how CloudFront supports scalable and secure applications.

**UTMorphos Team**

**Topic: "36 Hours with LotusHacks Hackathon"**

- Shared experiences from participating in the LotusHacks hackathon.
- Presented an AI-powered website template generation tool.
- Demonstrated how AI simplifies web design and development.

**Vy Lam**

**Topic: "Enterprise Grade Multi-Agent Systems"**

- Introduced enterprise multi-agent AI architectures.
- Shared implementation strategies and practical experiences.
- Explained real-world applications of AI agents in enterprise systems.

### Key Highlights

### 1. "Three Essential Elements for Modern Developers" – Presented by Master Hưng

- Understood the importance of building strong programming fundamentals.
- Learned that continuous learning and practical experience are essential for long-term career growth.
- Realized that developing solid skills should come before focusing on products.

### 2. "Why AI Fails Without Context" – Presented by Tịnh

- Learned that context is a critical factor in achieving high-quality AI responses.
- Understood how prompts, context, and memory work together in AI systems.
- Identified common mistakes when interacting with AI tools.

### 3. "Amazon Q Overview and Demo" – Presented by Anh Phạm

- Became familiar with Amazon Q and its AI-assisted development features.
- Observed real-world demonstrations of Amazon Q in software development.
- Learned how Amazon Q integrates with AWS services to improve productivity.

### 4. "CloudFront Fixed Pricing and Advanced Security" – Presented by Thịnh

- Learned how CloudFront pricing works and how fixed pricing simplifies cost management.
- Understood the purpose of advanced security features such as DDoS protection and Signed URLs.
- Gained a better understanding of CloudFront architecture and CDN optimization.

### 5. "36 Hours with LotusHacks Hackathon" – Presented by UTMorphos Team

- Learned about the challenges and experiences of participating in a hackathon.
- Understood how AI can automate website template generation from images.
- Recognized the importance of teamwork and rapid problem-solving during software development.

### 6. "Enterprise Grade Multi-Agent Systems" – Presented by Vy Lam

- Learned the basic architecture of enterprise multi-agent systems.
- Understood how AI agents collaborate to solve complex business problems.
- Gained insight into future trends in enterprise AI development.
